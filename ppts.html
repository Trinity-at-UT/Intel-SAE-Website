MICRO 2016

Staurday, October 15, 2016

BigBench, or TPCx-BigBench (TPCx-BB) , is a TPC Express benchmark for measuring the performance of Big Data Analytics Systems. We will be holding a three-hour tutorial on running BigBench with SAE during MICRO 2016. The whole tutorial will (tentatively) contain three stages:
Hour 1: explanation of Simics and SAE
Hour 2: introduction to BigBench
Hour 3: Simics/SAE hands-on tutorial
